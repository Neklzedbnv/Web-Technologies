Dessert Recipes

Sweet treats that make everyone happy.

Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake with frosting.

  1. Grease and flour two round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir all together.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. Bake 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Creamy cheesecake topped with fresh strawberries.

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
  2. Beat cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Bake for 55-60 minutes.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic cookies with gooey chocolate chips.

  1. Cream together butter and sugars.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Matcha Cheesecake

A creamy cheesecake infused with Japanese green tea matcha flavor.

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter.
  2. Beat cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Sift in matcha powder.
  4. Bake for 55-60 minutes.
  5. Cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes.

Lemon Mousse

Light and fluffy mousse with refreshing lemon flavor and whipped cream topping.

  1. Whisk lemon juice, sugar, and egg yolks.
  2. Heat the mixture over.
  3. Beat heavy cream.
  4. Fold the cream into the cooled lemon mixture.
